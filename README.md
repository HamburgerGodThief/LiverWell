# LiverWell
To help people prevent from the threat of fatty-liver,  Liverwell helps you accomplish the goal of 15-minute workout per day. Provides easy workouts, personal progress/weight tracking, and helpful health-related tips.
* Based on your work schedule, display home page with three different status: **Working**, **Resting** and **Before Sleep**, and sugget workouts accordingly.
* Easy workouts with claer and practical purpose
    * **Working**: 5-minute stretch exercises that can be done in office
    * **Resting**: easy training for time relaxing at home, e.g. the one you can do while watching tv, and the one you can do to ease back pain. Choose the workout duration according to your need.
    * **Before Sleep**: stretch exercies to help you relax and have better sleep.
* Knowledge: categorize tips with fatty-liver, health, and food for easier searching
* Progress tracking: show visualized personal progress in charts
